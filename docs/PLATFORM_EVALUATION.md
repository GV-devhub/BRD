# 🧩 Platform Evaluation – Wix vs WordPress vs Blogger  
*(Aligned with BRD Appendix)*

## Summary  
Three platforms were evaluated:

- **Wix** – Easy drag‑and‑drop  
- **WordPress** – Highly customizable  
- **Blogger** – Free SSL, zero cost, Google ecosystem  

**Final Selection:** Blogger

---

## Comparison Table

| Platform | Strengths | Limitations | Suitability |
|---------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Wix | Easy builder, templates | Ads, limited SEO, no free SSL | Small personal sites |
| WordPress | Full customization | Requires hosting + SSL | Long‑term professional sites |
| Blogger | Free SSL, free hosting | Limited customization | Learning documentation |

---

## Decision Rationale  
Blogger meets all BRD objectives:

- Zero cost  
- Free SSL  
- Fast setup  
- Google Analytics + Search Console  
- Low maintenance
