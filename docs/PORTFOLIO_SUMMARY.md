# 🌐 Aidivehub – Portfolio Summary

Aidivehub is a personal learning and documentation blog created to centralize daily learning, AI tool usage, SQL/Excel practice, analytics workflows, and productivity insights.

This summary is aligned with the official Business Requirements Document (BRD).

---

## 📘 Purpose (BRD Section 1)

- Centralize scattered learning notes  
- Build a professional online presence  
- Support future monetization  
- Maintain a cost‑free, secure platform  

---

## 🧠 Key Content Areas

- AI Tools  
- SQL & Excel  
- Analytics Workflows  
- Productivity & Learning  
- Microdose Mindset  
- Six Sigma  
- Digital Marketing  
- Health  

---

## 🔄 Process Flow (BRD Sections 11–14)

Learn → Draft → Format → SEO → Publish → Share → Track → Improve

---

## 🧩 Platform Decision (BRD Appendix)

**Selected Platform:** Blogger  
**Reason:** Free SSL, zero hosting cost, Google ecosystem, simple UI.
